What is OverTime?

For student-athletes searching for a job coming out of college, OverTime provides the only connection service tailored specifically to student athletes. It will allow student athletes to find a former student-athlete mentor to ease the transition coming out of college.

Features

Features

Multi-Platform

OverTime works on both desktop and mobile so students can connect anywhere and anytime.

Easy to Use

As soon as a student opens OverTime, he/she can find new connections or contact existing ones.

Database

Former NCAA D1 student athletes are willing to assist and connect student-athletes to opportunities.

Strong Team

The diverse team behind OverTime is committed to helping student-athletes find success.
